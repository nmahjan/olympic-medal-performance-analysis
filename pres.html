⚖️ Finding #2: Gender Participation Evolution

~0% Female Athletes (1896)
~45% Female Athletes (2016)

The Dramatic Transformation

  • 1896-1920: Women essentially excluded
  • 1920-1980: Slow, steady growth begins
  • 1980-2016: Rapid acceleration toward equality

Conclusion: This represents one of the most significant social changes in Olympic history, reflecting broader movements toward gender equality worldwide.
